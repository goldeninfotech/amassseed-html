Our Sales and Marketing Activities

Bangladesh is an agro-based country having immense potentiality. Organized efforts can take her agro-business to the apex of development. A group of committed agriculturists have come forward with a mission to augment sustainable development of agriculture through establishment of high tech agro-industries. They have established a group of business companies named “Krishibid Group” (KG) with the founder firm - Krishibid Firm Limited (KFL) being the holding company. The group started operation in 2001 having registered with the Registrar of Joint Stock Companies and Firms. The business of the group began with a very small capital of only 30 thousand taka and a small poultry farm that accommodated only some 3 thousand poultry birds. Now, it is a well-known and famous organization having 33 sister concerns and many other organizations and projects, in which some 5000 employees are working. This is the only nation wide business organization among the agriculturist professionals in Bangladesh. The KG has received a share money of Tk 2524 million from 6500 shareholders, against which an average dividend of 19% has been given since its inception. This is an unparalleled achievement. The shareholders have so far been paid back Tk 3158 million and their leftover share money is now Tk 2955 million. The group now possesses assets of Tk 30 thousand million which is about six times more than the liabilities. This has all become possible by the grace of Almighty, sincere and hard efforts of the management and maintenance of high standard business ethics.
